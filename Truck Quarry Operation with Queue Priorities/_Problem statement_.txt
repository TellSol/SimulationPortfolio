Problem Description:
____________________

A quarry operation consists of 4 20-tonner trucks and 2 50-tonner trucks moving between a shovelling and a crushing operation. 
Shovelling takes 2 hours and crushing takes 5 hours. Moving between the operations takes 1 hour. 

When a queue is formed in front of the crushing operation. The 50-tonner trucks always goes in front of the line. 


Run a simulation where you show have the 4 20-tonner trucks over time does not get to unload any raw material as the 50-tonners gradually takes over.
